Position Papers

Please submit position papers by April 17th, 2026 to be considered for research awards.

Deadline: April 17

What to Include

Please use font size 12, double spaced, Times New Roman Font with 1-inch margains.

Your position paper should be 1–2 pages and contain the following sections:

  • Relevant background and history of the topic
  • Previous UN actions
  • Your country's general stance on the topic
  • Proposed solutions and policy recommendations

*If you are in a spec-crisis committee, include global actions & character position rather than UN actions and country position.

Submission

Submit your position paper using the form below. Make sure to include your school name, committee, and country assignment.
